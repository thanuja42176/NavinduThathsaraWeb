About Me

Proudly shaped by the traditions and values of Ananda College, I carry forward a legacy of excellence, integrity, and service.

My Ananda Journey

My journey at Ananda College began in 2000, where I was immersed in an environment that valued not just academic excellence, but holistic development and character building. Founded in 1886, Ananda College's rich historical legacy and commitment to producing well-rounded individuals has profoundly shaped my worldview and approach to life.

The school's philosophy of "Wisdom and Virtue" became my personal mantra, guiding me through challenges and opportunities alike. The blend of traditional values with modern education at Ananda gave me a unique perspective that I carry into everything I do.

My Journey

From Ananda College to my current endeavors, here's the path that shaped my career and perspective.

2000-2012

Ananda College Education

My formative years at Ananda College laid the foundation for my future. I immersed myself in both academics and extracurriculars, participating in debates, sports, and cultural activities. The school's emphasis on discipline and character development shaped my approach to challenges.

2012-2016

University Education

Building on the strong foundation from Ananda College, I pursued higher education with the same values of excellence and perseverance. My university years were marked by academic achievements and leadership roles that expanded my horizons.

2016-2020

Early Career & Professional Growth

Entering the professional world, I carried forward the Ananda legacy of excellence and integrity. These years were crucial in establishing my professional identity while maintaining the core values instilled during my school days.

2020-Present

Leadership & Innovation

Currently focused on innovation and leadership in my field, I continue to draw inspiration from Ananda's philosophy of "Wisdom and Virtue." I actively participate in alumni activities and mentorship programs to give back to the institution that shaped me.
